The Best Movies

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Fast & Furious

This is one of my favorite movies. This movies series is largely concerned with street racing, heists, spies, and family.

Home Alone

Kevin McCallister, a boy who defends his suburban Chicago home from burglars after his family accidentally leaves him behind on their Christmas vacation to Paris.

Creed

Creed is a sports drama film about the son of former boxing champion Apollo Creed, who seeks out the help of his father's rival, Rocky Balboa, to train him and help him achieve his own boxing dreams.
